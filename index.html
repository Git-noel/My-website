Our Story

About MediCare Clinic

Serving the Nyeri community with quality, affordable healthcare since 2009.

Committed to Your Wellbeing

MediCare Clinic was founded in 2009 with a simple mission: to make quality healthcare accessible to everyone in Nyeri and the surrounding region. What started as a small general practice has grown into a full-service clinic with over 12 specialist doctors and a dedicated support team.

We believe that great healthcare is built on trust, transparency, and genuine human connection. Every patient who walks through our doors is treated with dignity and given the time and attention they deserve.

❤️

Compassionate Care

We treat every patient like family, ensuring a warm and supportive experience at every visit.

🎯

Clinical Excellence

Our team stays updated with the latest medical practices and evidence-based treatments.

🤝

Community First

We are proud members of the Nyeri community and are committed to improving local health outcomes.

💰

Affordable for All

We offer flexible payment plans and accept NHIF to ensure cost is never a barrier to care.
